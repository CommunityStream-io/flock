download Step 1: Request Your Instagram Data

First, you'll need to download your Instagram data. Don't worry - it's easier than it sounds!

1

Visit Instagram's Download Page

Click the button below or go to Instagram's settings:

open_in_new Request Instagram Data
2

Select Your Data

Choose "Download Your Information" and select:

  • Date range: All time (or your preferred range)
  • Format: JSON
  • Media quality: High (recommended for best results)
3

Wait for the Email

Instagram will process your request. This can take anywhere from a few minutes to 48 hours depending on how much data you have. You'll receive an email when it's ready!

4

Download Your Archive

When you receive the email from Instagram, click the download link. Here's what the email will look like:

info Save the downloaded ZIP file somewhere you can easily find it - you'll need it in the next step!

Why use an App Password?

For your security, we strongly recommend using an App Password instead of your main Bluesky password. This way, if you ever want to revoke Flock's access, you can do so without changing your main password.

1

Go to Bluesky Settings

Log into your Bluesky account and navigate to Settings â†’ App Passwords

2

Create New App Password

Click "Add App Password" and give it a name like "Flock Migration"

3

Save Your Password

Copy the generated password immediately - you won't be able to see it again! You'll use this during the authentication step in Flock.

Security Best Practices

  • check_circle Never share your App Password with others
  • check_circle Store it securely (password manager recommended)
  • check_circle Revoke it after your migration is complete if you won't use Flock again
  • check_circle Create separate App Passwords for different apps
 speed Understanding Rate Limits

Bluesky has rate limits to ensure platform stability. Here's what you need to know:

schedule What are rate limits?

Rate limits control how many posts you can create within a certain time period. This prevents spam and keeps the platform running smoothly for everyone.

hourglass_empty How does this affect my migration?

If you have a large Instagram archive, Flock will automatically pace your uploads to stay within Bluesky's limits. This means your migration might take several hours or even days for very large archives.

Don't worry! Flock tracks your progress and can resume if interrupted. You can safely close the app and come back later.

tips_and_updates Tips for a smooth migration
  • Start your migration when you have time to let it run
  • Keep your device awake and connected to power
  • Ensure stable internet connection
  • Consider migrating in smaller batches if you prefer
  • Be patient - good things take time!
